Welcome to the Southeast Collegiate Cyber Defense Website

A Division of the NCCDC


The 2018 SECCDC event will be held on Kennesaw State University's campus over its spring break. From April 2nd - 3rd, the regional competition event will host eight top performing teams from 8am to 6pm in the main campus's Burruss Building. The winning team of the on-site regional competition will represent the Southeast Region at the National Collegiate Cyber Defense Competition, which will be be held from April 13th - 15th 2018 in San Antonio.

The SECCDC is proud to invite the following teams to the on-site regional:

Clemson University Kennesaw State University Mercer University University of Central Florida
University of Florida University of North Carolina, Charlotte University of North Carolina, Wilmington University of South Alabama


The SECCDC is conducted by the Coles College of Business Center for Information Security Education. The regional competition is managed by the following Kennesaw State University Faculty members:

Dr. Mike Whitman
Dr. Herb Mattord
Mr. James Blanton
Director, Event Coordinator Associate Director, Event Co-Coordinator Red Team Coordinator, Co-Architect
