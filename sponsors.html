Contributions Make SECCDC a Success

Ways You Can Help Us Grow


Direct financial contribution:
All donations to the SECCDC are tax deductible, and a receipt will be provided on request. Financial contributions can be made through Kennesaw State University's ePay webpage.

In-kind donations:
Vendors wishing to supply hardware, software or services should contact Dr. Whitman. Please note, we only accept donations that directly contribute to the conduct of the competition. We humbly request that if licensed products are provided, that sufficient time is provided to install, test, and configure the products to support the competition. All in-kind donations must be received by January 31 for the competition cycle to be of any value to the competition.

Volunteers:
The competition coordinators are always looking for personnel to assist as team judges and red team pen testers. However, we do not typically count employee time as an in-kind donation for purposes of event sponsorship. We welcome those who donate their time or the time of their employees. However, we cannot provide the benefits and recognition to individuals or organizations who volunteer employee time, as we do for other donation categories, except in very rare circumstances.

For those organizations or individuals interested in sponsoring multiple regional CCDC events (4 or more), please contact the national CCDC coordinators.

If your organization includes the SECCDC as part of its sponsorship package, we will provide a 15% discount on the sponsorship costs associated with the following levels. In addition, National Multi-Regional Sponsors receive special recognition and thanks in all signage, publications, presentations and documentation.

Regional Sponsorship Levels:

Platinum $10,000
  • Receive notice in all event signage, publications, presentations, and registration materials
  • Separate event signage prominently displayed throughout the event
  • Reception event dedicated to their organization with appropriate signage OR event gifts distributed with their corporate information***
  • May include materials inside team packets
  • Up to six representatives at the event to interact with participants
  • Recruitment table at reception and in building atrium during competition
Gold $5,000
  • Receive notice in all event signage, publications, presentations, and registration materials
  • Dedicated event meals with appropriate signage
  • May include materials in team packets
  • Up to four representatives at the event to interact with participants
  • Recruitment table at reception
Silver $2,500
  • Receive notice in all event signage, publications, presentations, and registration materials
  • May include materials in team packets
  • Up to two representatives at the event to interact with participants
Bronze $1,000
  • Receive notice in all event signage, publications, presentations, and registration materials
  • May request copies of team resumes after the event (no participation in on-site recruiting activities)
Sponsor $250
  • Receive a link on the event website
  • Mention during opening and closing presentation

***Sufficient advanced notice required for all imprinted items

Contact Dr. Whitman for details and payment options

Special thanks to the National CCDC and the University of Texas, San Antonio's Center for Infrastructure Assurance and Security for their endorsement and support for this competition.
